\[ \int_{-2}^{3} \left( \frac{3}{4}x^2 + 1 \right) dx = \left[ \frac{1}{4}x^3 + x \right]_{-2}^{3} \]

\[ = \left( \frac{1}{4} \cdot 3^3 + 3 \right) - \left( \frac{1}{4} \cdot (-2)^3 + (-2) \right) = \left( \frac{27}{4} + 3 \right) - \left( -2 + (-2) \right) \]

\[ = \frac{27}{4} + 3 + 2 + 2 = \boxed{13.75} \]
